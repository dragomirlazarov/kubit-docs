title: "Alerts, Roles, and Packages"
---

- **How do I trigger a Slack alert for an "Infinite Loop"?** Recursive loops can drain your budget in minutes. You can save a query that flags traces with an unusual number of steps and set an **Anomaly Trigger** to ping your developers in Slack instantly.
  - **See more:** [Atlas Dashboards & Alerts](https://kubit.mintlify.app/dashboard)
- **What are the different User Roles in Atlas?** Control who can edit your data logic. We offer roles ranging from **Admin** (full control) to **Governor** (dictionary management) and **Viewer** (read-only reporting), ensuring your semantic layer stays protected.
  - **See more:** [Quick Start Guide](https://kubit.mintlify.app/quick-start-guide)
- **What is included in the "Free Tier" vs. "Pro Tier"?** The Free Tier is perfect for local testing of your first AI agent. The **Pro Tier** is designed for production, offering unlimited event tracking, advanced alerts, and full team collaboration tools.
  - **See more:** [Quick Start Guide](https://kubit.mintlify.app/quick-start-guide)
- **How do I set a "Hard Limit" on LLM spend alerts?** Financial governance is built-in. By setting a metric-based alert on your `Estimated Spend`, Atlas act as a 24/7 auditor, alerting you the moment your token usage hits a pre-defined budget threshold.
  - **See more:** [Atlas Dashboards & Alerts](https://kubit.mintlify.app/dashboard)
- **Can I restrict data access by team?** Yes. For enterprise security, you can create separate workspaces or permission sets. This ensures the Finance team sees the ROI, while the Engineering team sees the deep technical error and trace logs.
  - **See more:** [Quick Start Guide](https://kubit.mintlify.app/quick-start-guide)
