title: 'Building Your First "Cost vs. Performance" Query'
---

**The Goal:** Answering the most common stakeholder question.

**The Walkthrough:** Use the **Query** tool to select `Total Tokens` and `Latency`. Use the **Breakdown** feature to slice the data by `Model Engine` (e.g., comparing GPT-4o vs. Claude 3.5).

**Key Outcome:** "Identify your most expensive (and slowest) models instantly."
