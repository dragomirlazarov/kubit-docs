title: "Designing a Behavioral Funnel (Intent to Action)"
---

**The Goal:** Measuring if the AI is actually useful.

**The Walkthrough:** Create a 3-step funnel: 1. `User Prompt`, 2. `Agent Execution`, 3. `Positive Feedback`. Show how to identify where users are dropping off or "undoing" the AI's work.

**Key Outcome:** "Identify the 'Utility Gap' in your AI features."
