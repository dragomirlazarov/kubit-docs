---
title: 'Mapping your first "AI Session"'
---

- **The Goal:** Moving from isolated API calls to a behavioral story.
- **Content:** Learn how to use a `session_id` or `trace_id` in the **Atlas Dictionary** to group multiple LLM exchanges into a single user journey. This article explains how Atlas stitches together prompts and responses to show you the "Success Rate" of an entire interaction rather than just one-off calls.
