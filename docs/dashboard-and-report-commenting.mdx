# Commenting on Dashboards

Enhance collaboration by commenting on individual Reports or the entire Dashboard.

## Leave a Comment

1. Click the **Speech Bubble Icon** at the bottom of your Dashboard.
2. Click on a Report or a blank space to comment on the Report widget or the Dashboard.
3. Type your comment and confirm with the **Blue Arrow Icon**.

![](/images/docs/27212c3f98431f1236d9ef2a150f2d4236f2387d9c4c39cace1088dad840aeaf-leave_a_comment.gif)

## View All Comments

1. Click the **Speech Bubble Icon** in the sidebar.
2. Select the Comment thread you wish to view. Clicking a comment on a Report will navigate to that Report.

![](/images/docs/02e3ae7e98cde024487c994d540b35e71c984e35547f4bf4f49f4626161e7810-view_all_comments.gif)

### Thread Indicator

A red dot on the sidebar's **Comments Icon** indicates new comments.

![Thread Indicator](/images/docs/997e69a6ea73b918ff22e694b63fb01d18e0e6c91cc8f1e62d9682eba4b05ed2-image.png)

## Respond to a Comment

1. Click the **Speech Bubble Icon** on a Report or in the sidebar.
2. Type your response in the text box below the original comment.
3. Confirm with the **Blue Arrow Icon**.

![](/images/docs/7ef007b091f4b3bb3f6faa2008e29cbcee24c5210c08134c08d72b282a1b1482-respond_to_a_comment.gif)

## Edit a Comment

1. Click the **Speech Bubble Icon** in the sidebar.
2. Select the Comment thread to edit.
3. Click the three dots next to the Comment, select **Edit**, and modify your message.

![](/images/docs/b353afcb88fd54ccb3d2b380142953f7d9aeb42e2adb9b8f9290b0e36e22e6b9-edit_a_comment.gif)

## Resolve a Thread of Comments

1. Open a Comment.
2. Click the **Circled Checkmark Icon** to resolve the comment chain.

![](/images/docs/0f011019cdd437934c411fe94e5c8b2cf0907fe940672d0afe0039ab0ede207f-resolve_a_thread_of_comments.gif)

## Delete a Comment

1. Open a Comment thread.
2. Click the three dots next to any comment (except the first one) and select **Delete**.

![](/images/docs/2ace746dae2bbe3d7fb4ba2e21347d526cd9be41dc8e8cd97014385e8d742f81-delete_a_comment.gif)

## Add Images

1. Create a new comment.
2. Click the **Image Icon**.
3. Upload your image with the **Blue Arrow Icon**.

![](/images/docs/604473e4bdcbab027d35f84292a76c2ca395315736b3541a48b4bb3cdf457f37-add_images.gif)

# Slack Enabled Commenting

Mention other Kubit users to notify them via Slack, provided their Slack integration is set up. For setup, refer to our [Integrate Kubit with Slack](https://dash.readme.com/project/kubit/v1.0/docs/integrate-kubit-with-slack) guide.

## Set Up a Slack Channel for Comment Updates

1. As a Dashboard Manager, click the **Gear Icon** in the comment sidebar.
2. Select a channel to receive comment updates.

## Mention Other Users

1. Create a comment.
2. Use the @ icon to mention a user. Notifications will be sent to their Slack.

<Info>
  ### **Note:** Once mentioned, users will continue to receive thread updates.
</Info>

## Slack Commenting Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues with Slack notifications, follow these steps:

1. Remove the App Integration from Slack by clicking the `X` in the `Apps` section.
2. In Kubit, go to `Personal Settings` via the **User Icon**.
3. Disconnect and reconnect Slack in the `Slack Integration` section.

![](/images/docs/d3c31f014e3e0c0e1a9f64c9162b4fd3e95d51d4c665a94f7dfcd16826efaf7c-slack_commenting_troubleshooting_2.png) ![](/images/docs/51627991e39db857ddad811efcfed15aacc6608e7735effda6ba683849375f85-slack_commenting_troubleshooting_3.png)

Reconnect to Slack and test commenting functionality.
