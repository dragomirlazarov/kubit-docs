# History

The `History` feature provides users with a persistent way of retaining executed Reports. After you execute a Report, it automatically appears in your `History` in the left-navigation panel so you can easily navigate back to your recently executed Reports.

![](/images/docs/a95fe136d5dc30ba88828639d062c706b23375ce913017d54c0a45df26436a63-analysis_history.png)

# Drafts

The `Drafts` feature provides users with a persistent, session-independent way to retain unfinished report work. Drafts are automatically saved when you begin creating or editing a Report without executing it. Your Drafts appear right on top of your `History`, are capped at the 10 most recent, and persist across sessions. Drafts help you maintain workflow continuity, minimize accidental loss, and quickly return to work in progress.

![](/images/docs/4df9676671a3de6dd3bee22dc47a73d80a933ddfb6ee45cf3d080c8ff217cd4e-analysis_drafts.png)
