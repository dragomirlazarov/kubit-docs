Lumos is Kubit’s AI-powered analytics engine built to make data exploration intuitive, fast, and transparent. Whether you want reports, cohorts, explanations, or smart suggestions - just ask in plain English. Lumos is designed for teams empowered by data who want answers, without the complexity of SQL or rigid tooling.

# What Can Lumos Do?

## Explain with AI

After any report runs, click the ✨ icon to get automatic summaries, anomaly detection, key metrics, and action recommendations - all powered by Lumos. It uses only aggregated data and metadata to keep everything secure. Learn more → [Lumos Explain with AI](https://docs.kubit.ai/docs/kubit-explain-with-ai)

## Natural Language Report Creation

Ask a question in the report builder or fullscreen "Ask anything" box - Lumos translates it into a full query with events, filters, and timeframes, then builds and executes visual report components. You can refine the output iteratively via follow-up prompts. Learn more → [Natural Language Report Creation guide](https://docs.kubit.ai/docs/natural-language-report-creation)

## Natural Language Cohort Creation

In the Cohort section, type definitions like “users who purchased ≥ 4 items last month and signed up for email,” and Lumos translates your language into cohort logic, then lets you review, save, or iterate. Learn more → [Natural Language Cohort Creation guide](https://docs.kubit.ai/docs/natural-language-cohort-creation)

# How Can I Use Lumos?

Lumos is available to all Enterprise customers - if you'd like to upgrade to Lumos please reach out to your Kubit Customer Success Manager

# Tips to Making Lumos Work for Your Team

* Be specific in prompts: specify events, filters, timeframe, and segmentation (e.g. “US users who clicked subscribe button last month”).
  * Review our [Prompt Best Practices Guide](https://docs.kubit.ai/docs/prompt-best-practices)
* Enrich your metadata by adding event & field descriptions to make Lumos' decisions more accurate.
* Iterate often and use follow-up prompts like “break that down by country” or “only for new users”.

# Who Benefits from Lumos?

* PMs, analysts, and business users who want to go from question to insight fast and confidently.
* Teams seeking to reduce redundant query effort and streamline reporting workflows.
* Organizations focused on governance and transparency - Lumos builds using data from your secure data warehouse and doesn't use your data to train other models.
