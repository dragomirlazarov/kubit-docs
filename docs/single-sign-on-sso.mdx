## Introduction

By integrating SSO with Kubit’s self-service analytics, your team can have fine control over the users in your organization who can sign in to Kubit. With SSO, users sign in using their existing credentials.

<Check>
  ### SSO to help control access

  The SSO approach eliminates another set of username and password credentials to be remembered, simplifies user management, and can also improve security through your existing multi-factor authentication (MFA).
</Check>

Depending on your SSO configuration, check out one of the following guides:

1. [Google Workspace](doc:google-workspace)
2. [Okta](doc:okta)
3. [Azure Microsoft Entra ID (Active Directory)](doc:azure-active-directory-ad)
4. [AWS IAM Identity Center](doc:aws-iam-identity-center)
