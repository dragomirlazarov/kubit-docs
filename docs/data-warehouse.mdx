Kubit seamlessly integrates with leading data warehouse solutions like Snowflake, Databricks, BigQuery, and ClickHouse. This direct connection ensures you have real-time access to your data without the need for complex data transfers or batch processing. By connecting directly, you maintain full control over your data, ensuring a single source of truth.

## Direct Connect

Kubit offers a secure, warehouse-native integration, allowing direct access to your cloud data warehouse. This means no data is copied or transferred, eliminating the need for engineering work to create ETL or batch jobs, and avoiding additional storage requirements. With this setup, Kubit provides instant insights from real-time data, maintaining a single source of truth.

To enable Direct Connect, provide Kubit with a user account that has read-only permissions to query your data. For detailed setup instructions, refer to the specific guides for each data warehouse:

* [Snowflake](doc:snowflake-direct)
* [BigQuery](/docs/bigquery-direct)
* [Databricks](doc:databricks-direct)
* [ClickHouse](doc:clickhouse-direct)

For enhanced security, consider adding Kubit's IP addresses to your firewall settings. See [Kubit IP addresses](doc:kubit-ip-addresses) for the list.
