# What are Scratchpads?

Scratchpads are your go-to space for gathering related insights. You can add text, images, Reports, Dashboards, and Cohorts to create a comprehensive story around your investigatory workflows or daily tasks without the need to build a full Dashboard.

# Create a Scratchpad

To access the Scratchpad activity, locate the right-hand sidebar and click the second icon. This opens the Scratchpad activity. Click **Create Scratchpad** to start your first Scratchpad. To rename it, double-click the title and enter a new name.

![](/images/docs/5cf7d031a917bcc1b29166c8886212202a1aefed98dbf143f3a52240bd4a700a-create_a_scratchpad.gif)

You can expand or minimize Scratchpads by clicking and dragging their edge.

![](/images/docs/23e6cadc6dfdb738bfaee62b0901825fb36e503ee07d7f573d725ccc273cb51d-clicking_and_dragging_scratchpad.gif)

# Adding Content to a Scratchpad

## Adding Images, Reports, Dashboards, or Cohorts

To add content, use the plus (+) button. Selecting an image opens your file picker, while choosing Reports, Dashboards, or Cohorts takes you to Universal Search.

<Check>
  ### **Pro-tip:**

  Use the images option to display GIFs in Scratchpad.
</Check>

You can also add Reports by opening the Report, clicking the **Add To** button, and selecting a Scratchpad. Reports added this way appear at the bottom, while those added with the plus (+) button appear at your chosen location.

![](/images/docs/40048aeb3d2288855de616dccf404bd77f059bda5bd9c7d6be94221f558b590d-Adding_content_to_a_scratchpad_1.gif)

Dashboards and Cohorts can only be added via the plus (+) button and selection from Universal Search. These appear as links, providing quick access to frequently used resources.

![](/images/docs/3847eac3cc63758fea9662928f32a04cf7858cfcc7b2f26bf311f9f276ac0d5b-adding_content_to_a_scratchpad_2.gif)

## Adding Text

Text can be inserted anywhere between images, Reports, Dashboards, and Cohorts. Wherever you see a plus (+) button, you can add text. Formatting options include bold, italics, underline, highlight, headers, lists, horizontal line breaks, and links.

![](/images/docs/ef2f40e9af1d92502d7f2291ffdb528e07e41d368822d118eb89d8411be29593-adding_text_to_a_scratchpad.gif)

# Sharing Scratchpads

Scratchpads are private by default but can be shared with others. Sharing is similar to sharing a Dashboard, but others can only be editors, allowing them to update the original Scratchpad.

![](/images/docs/fea94b21d6dd8324cbf2d3b632e8585fd0f73f8bc5db6e8bd87e70de046ddd75-sharing_scratchpads_1.gif)

When shared with you, a Scratchpad appears in a new section in the selection drop-down, helping you organize Scratchpads created by yourself and others.

![](/images/docs/5cd48e8eefa9858f0230c6feeb509fe5948779b2965df3fcc9cf145cd843a015-sharing_scratchpads_2.png)
