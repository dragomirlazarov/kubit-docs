## Overview

Leverage your customer data effectively by integrating with leading Customer Data Platforms (CDPs). Kubit seamlessly connects with the following platforms to enhance your user behavior analysis:

* **[Segment](doc:segment):** Streamline your data collection and management.
* **[Snowplow](doc:snowplow):** Gain granular insights with event-level data.
* **[mParticle](doc:mparticle):** Unify your data for a comprehensive view of customer interactions.
* **[RudderStack](doc:rudderstack):** Simplify data routing and processing.

These integrations ensure you can analyze customer behavior without the need to move data, maintaining both efficiency and accuracy.
