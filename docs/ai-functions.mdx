---
title: "AI Functions in Derived Fields"
description: "Leverage warehouse-native AI functions from Snowflake, BigQuery, and Databricks to analyze unstructured text data like customer reviews and product descriptions directly within your Kubit derived fields."
---

If you have unstructured data such as customer reviews or product descriptions you can now leverage it in Kubit using the built-in warehouse AI Functions.

Supported warehouses and their corresponding AI Functions references:

* [Snowflake](https://docs.snowflake.com/en/user-guide/snowflake-cortex/aisql) (available only in [select regions](https://docs.snowflake.com/en/user-guide/snowflake-cortex/aisql#label-cortex-llm-availability))
* [BigQuery](https://docs.cloud.google.com/bigquery/docs/generative-ai-overview#managed_ai_functions) (you may need to [enable AI Functions](https://docs.cloud.google.com/bigquery/docs/generate-text-tutorial-gemini#grant-permissions) for the service account used by Kubit first)
* [Databricks](https://docs.cloud.google.com/bigquery/docs/generative-ai-overview#managed_ai_functions) (still in preview, you might need to enable from [manage previews](https://docs.databricks.com/aws/en/admin/workspace-settings/manage-previews))
