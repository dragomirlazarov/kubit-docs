- **The Goal:** Going from raw tables to a live visual pulse in under 5 minutes.

You’ll learn how to type a natural language command like _"Show me total tokens used by model type today"_ to generate your first chart. This article covers how to pin that chart to a dashboard and share it with your team to monitor real-time Agent performance.
