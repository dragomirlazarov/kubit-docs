title: 'Mastering the "Lumos" Natural Language Query'
---

**The Goal:** Turning anyone into a Data Analyst.

**Content:** A guide on how to talk to Atlas. It covers the "Do’s and Don’ts" of prompting Lumos, such as: _"Show me the average latency per model type last week"_ vs. _"Which prompts cost us the most?"_ It also explains how to use the **"See SQL"** feature to verify the underlying Kubit-powered logic.
