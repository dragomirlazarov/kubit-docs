**The Goal:** Teaching Atlas what your raw data means.

**The Walkthrough:** Open a raw table with messy JSON. Show how to "Import" a column like `request_body` and map its nested fields (e.g., `model_id`, `tokens`) to standard Atlas metrics.

**Key Outcome:** "Turn cryptic JSON into readable AI metrics."
