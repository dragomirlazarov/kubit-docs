---
title: "Login to Kubit"
---

Sign in to Kubit using Single Sign-On (SSO) or an invite email with your temporary password, and set up your account with a secure password and multi-factor authentication.

### **What you need**

- Your Kubit organization URL. (Most teams use: `https://<company>.kubit.ai`)
- One of the following:
  - Single Sign-On (SSO), if your admin enabled it.
  - An invite email with a temporary password.

### **Sign in with SSO**

1. Open your organization’s Kubit URL.
2. Click **Login** to proceed with your SSO provider.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-3.png)
</Frame>

### **Sign In with an Invite Email (Username + Temporary Password)**

1. Open your organization’s Kubit URL.
2. Copy the temporary password from your invite email.
3. Click the **Log In** link in the invite email, or paste the password into the login page and continue.
4. Enter your credentials and click **Login now**.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-3.png)
</Frame>

### **First-Time Login: Set Your Password**

On your first login, Kubit will prompt you to set a new password:

1. Enter the temporary password from the invite email.
2. Create a new strong password and save it.

> 📘 **Trouble logging in?**
>
> Ensure you copied only the password, with no trailing spaces or other characters.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-3.png)
</Frame>

### **If MFA is Enabled**

If your environment requires multi-factor authentication (MFA), you will be prompted to set it up during login.

### **Troubleshooting**

**I cannot log in with the invite password.**

- Ensure you copied only the password, with no extra spaces at the end.

**Not sure which login method to use.**

- If you see an SSO option, use Login.
- If you received an invite email, use the temporary password and complete the first-time password setup.
