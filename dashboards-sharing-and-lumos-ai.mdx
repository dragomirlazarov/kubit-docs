---
title: "Dashboards, Sharing & Lumos AI"
---

- **How do I use Lumos AI to build a report from scratch?** Lumos removes the SQL barrier. You can type natural language questions into the search bar, and Atlas will generate the complex warehouse query and the visualization for you in real-time.
  - **See more:** [Lumos AI Overview](https://kubit.mintlify.app/test)
- **Can I add "Global Filters" to a Dashboard?** Consistency is key. Global Filters allow you to change the view for an entire dashboard (e.g., filtering for "Enterprise Customers" or "Production Environment") with a single click across all charts.
  - **See more:** [Atlas Dashboards](https://kubit.mintlify.app/dashboard)
- **How do I share a live dashboard with a non-Atlas user?** Stakeholders shouldn't have to log in to see progress. You can enable Public Sharing links or schedule automated exports to be sent to your team's email or Slack channel every Monday morning.
  - **See more:** [Atlas Dashboards](https://kubit.mintlify.app/dashboard)
- **What does "Pin to Dashboard" do?** When you find a specific insight in the Query tool—like a cost spike in Claude 3.5—you can "Pin" it to a permanent dashboard so your team can monitor that specific metric 24/7.
  - **See more:** [Atlas Dashboards](https://kubit.mintlify.app/dashboard)
- **Can Lumos explain "why" a metric changed?** Lumos isn't just a search bar; it's an analyst. You can ask it to "Break down the latency spike from yesterday," and it will automatically analyze your model providers and user cohorts to find the culprit.
  - **See more:** [Lumos AI Overview](https://kubit.mintlify.app/test)
